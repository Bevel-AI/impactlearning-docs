# Welcome to Impact Learning

**Impact** is an AI-powered platform that helps educators, trainers, and content creators build professional e-learning content through natural conversation. Describe what you want, and watch your ideas transform into polished slide decks, interactive courses, and learning experiences.

## What Can You Create?

<Cards>

  </Card>
  <Card title="Course Platforms" href="/docs/features/courses">

  <Card title="Interactive Content" href="/docs/features/interactive">

  </Card>

## How It Works

1. **Start a conversation** — Describe your learning content in plain language
2. **Watch it come to life** — AI generates professional slides and courses in real-time
3. **Refine and iterate** — Make changes through conversation until it's perfect
4. **Publish and share** — Deploy your content for learners to access

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Conversational Creation** | Build content by describing what you need — no coding required |
| **Live Preview** | See your slides and courses instantly as they're generated |
| **Multi-Modal Content** | Add images, audio narration, videos, and interactive elements |
| **Professional Design** | Every slide follows e-learning best practices with accessible colors and layouts |
| **Template Library** | Start from curated templates for courses, slide decks, and dashboards |
| **Persistent Projects** | Your work is automatically saved and accessible across sessions |

## Who Is Impact For?

- **Educators & Trainers** — Create engaging course content and presentations without technical skills
- **Corporate L&D Teams** — Build internal training materials and onboarding programs
- **Course Creators** — Design and publish professional online learning platforms
- **Content Developers** — Generate educational materials with AI-powered assistance
