# Welcome to Impact Learning

**Impact** is an AI-powered platform that helps educators, trainers, and content creators build engaging learning experiences through natural conversation. Describe what you want, and watch your ideas transform into interactive courses, assessments, and immersive learning environments.

## How It Works

1. **Start a conversation** — Describe your learning experience in plain language
2. **Add source materials** — Upload PDFs, images (PNG, JPG, GIF, WebP), Markdown files, or videos (MP4, WebM, YouTube links)
3. **Watch it come to life** — AI generates professional content in real-time
4. **Refine and iterate** — Make changes through conversation until it's perfect
5. **Publish and share** — Deploy your content directly or export as SCORM packages for your LMS
